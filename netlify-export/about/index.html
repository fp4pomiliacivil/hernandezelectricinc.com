About Us

Back in 2005, Trino Hernandez established Electrical Services with one goal in mind: to give great electrical services that meet the highest value of work. From that day onwards, he never settled for less. Because lighting up our community matters, and so does the safety of our customers.

All of our electricians are experienced in commercial and residential electric work. It is about making sure that you feel confident with your choice, and know you are getting the safest solution in the field. We put pride into serving Mendocino and surrounding counties.

Two generations of learning, consulting, training, gaining skills, and experience. We are a family business, and we treat you as family, too. We handle every request with the utmost care and professionalism.
